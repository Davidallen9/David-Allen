A Recipe for tomato sauce

Making the perfect tomato sauce requires three things

    I. Top quality ingrediants

    II. Care and attention to detail

    III. Endless patience

Tomato Sauce Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 gallon water
  • 1lb Italian sausage
  • 1 large onion (finley chopped)
  • 6 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 tin of crushed plum tomatoes
  • 1 cup of tomato puree
  • 13⁄4 tablespoons sugar
  • A big splash of olive oil
  • A pinch of dried oregano
  • salt and pepper

Cooking instructions

Heat the olive in a large sauce pot. Fry the garlic in the olive oil, then drop the chopped the chopped onions, continue to fry until the onions are soft and have started to colour. Add the Italian sausage in the same pot an brown sausage on both sides then remove1 ⁄2 the sausage and reserve. pour 1⁄2 a gallon of boil in water over the remaining half of the sausages and boil for 10-12 minutes until done; remove and reserve. repeat this process fort he second half of the sausages(in reserve).

Add tin of tomatoes, tomato puree, garlic, the dried tomatoes 220° or gas mark 7, until most of the water has evaporated (about 3 cups left) and the olive oil starts to appear around the edge of the pan. Finish with salt and pepper to taste. pour this delicous sauce over pasta of your choice. Enjoy!!!
